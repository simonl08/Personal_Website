Games

One of my main hobbies is playing games mainly on PC at the moment but also consoles too from time to time. I pretty much grew up playing games. One of the first online games I played was 'Adventure quest', 'RuneScape' and 'World of Warcraft' in the early 2000s. This led up to RTS style games such as 'The Lord of the Rings: The Battle for Middle-earth' and also 'StarCraft'. Then I pretty much got into MMORPG's again with Guild Wars 2, Black Desert Online and revisiting previous MMO's again. I do play a variety of different games rather than just sticking to one but depends on what I feel like doing honestly. Some examples of other games I play are single player games like 'The Witcher' franchise, dark souls (probably one of my favourite franchise), your standard fps games and some JRPG.
